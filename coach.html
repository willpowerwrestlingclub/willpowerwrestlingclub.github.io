Owner & Head Coach

Damir Zakhartdinov


Damir Zakhartdinov is the owner and club founder of Willpower Wrestling Club since 2011.

Coach Damir has 14 years of international career for the Uzbekistan National Team.

During his carrier he had successfull appearence at 3 Olympic Games, 3 World Championships and 2 Asian Games.

Coach Damir moved to the United States to achieve his dream to become a World Class wrestling instructor. He opened his own club, where he can teach others what he does the best.

Before moving to Brooklyn and starting his own Willpower Wrestling Club, Damir coached children at Alabama's Cadet and Junior National team. After, Damir coached at Perry High School in Ohio, a top ten U.S. wrestling program.

Today Coach Damir trains wrestlers who earned multiple awards in wrestling in state of New York and in others states.
